7-Day Edinburgh Itinerary

This plan gives you a full experience of Edinburgh’s culture, nature, and history over 7 days. Each day includes locations with approximate start and end times.

Day 1: Old Town Wonders (9:00 AM – 6:00 PM | 9 hours)

Start with Edinburgh Castle, walk the Royal Mile, and explore historic spots like St Giles’ Cathedral and Camera Obscura.

Day 2: Nature & Museums (10:00 AM – 5:00 PM | 7 hours)

Visit the National Museum of Scotland, enjoy the Royal Botanic Garden, and explore art at the National Gallery.

Day 3: Scenic Walks & Views (8:00 AM – 4:00 PM | 8 hours)

Hike Arthur’s Seat, explore Calton Hill and Dean Village, and relax by the Water of Leith.

Day 4: Museums & Mystery (10:00 AM – 6:00 PM | 8 hours)

Explore Surgeons’ Hall, childhood nostalgia at the Museum of Childhood, and whisky tasting at the Scotch Whisky Experience.

Day 5: Hidden Gems & History (11:00 AM – 5:00 PM | 6 hours)

Visit the Writers’ Museum, Greyfriars Kirkyard, and uncover the secrets of Mary King’s Close.

Day 6: Waterfront & Markets (12:00 PM – 7:00 PM | 7 hours)

Spend time in the Grassmarket, enjoy Leith Shore’s vibe, and tour the Royal Yacht Britannia.

Day 7: Culture & Farewell Views (10:00 AM – 8:00 PM | 10 hours)

See the Scottish Parliament, explore modern art, stroll through Princes Street Gardens, and watch the sunset from Calton Hill.
